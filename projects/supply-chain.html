Supply Chain Optimization

SQL Python Power BI

The Problem

A global manufacturing company was struggling with inefficiencies in their supply chain that were directly impacting their bottom line. They faced several critical challenges:

  • Excessive inventory levels at some locations, while experiencing stockouts at others
  • Poor visibility into inventory movement across their network of warehouses and distribution centers
  • Inconsistent data across multiple disconnected systems, making it difficult to track inventory accurately
  • Rising logistics costs due to unoptimized transportation routes and emergency shipping needs
  • Inability to predict demand patterns, leading to overproduction or underproduction
  • No systematic way to identify bottlenecks in the supply chain process

The company needed a comprehensive solution that would provide end-to-end visibility of their supply chain, optimize inventory levels, and reduce logistics costs while ensuring product availability to meet customer demand.

The Solution

I developed an integrated ETL pipeline and analytics dashboard solution to address the supply chain challenges:

  • Created an ETL pipeline using Python and SQL to consolidate and clean data from multiple sources including ERP systems, warehouse management systems, and transportation management systems
  • Implemented data quality checks to identify and correct inconsistencies in inventory data across different systems
  • Developed a real-time inventory tracking system that provided visibility across all locations
  • Built a Power BI dashboard with role-based views for different stakeholders, from executives to warehouse managers
  • Created predictive models for demand forecasting using time series analysis and machine learning algorithms
  • Integrated logistics data to optimize transportation routes and shipping schedules
  • Implemented inventory optimization models to determine optimal safety stock levels based on demand variability and lead times
  • Developed automated alerts for potential stockouts or excess inventory situations

The solution provided a unified view of the entire supply chain with actionable insights for decision-makers at all levels of the organization.

The Impact

The supply chain optimization solution delivered significant measurable improvements across multiple areas:

12%

Annual cost savings in logistics and inventory

22%

Reduction in average inventory levels

35%

Decrease in stockout incidents

Beyond these quantitative improvements, the project also delivered several strategic benefits:

  • Improved decision-making with real-time visibility into the entire supply chain
  • Enhanced ability to respond to market changes with more accurate demand forecasting
  • Reduced lead times for order fulfillment, improving customer satisfaction
  • Optimized working capital through better inventory management
  • Identified and eliminated bottlenecks in the supply chain process
  • Created a foundation for continuous improvement through data-driven insights
  • Enabled more accurate planning for resource allocation and capacity management

The supply chain optimization solution has become a critical strategic asset for the company, supporting their goals for operational efficiency and customer satisfaction while significantly reducing costs.
