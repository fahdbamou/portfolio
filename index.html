About Me

I am a Business Intelligence Data Analyst with expertise in transforming complex data into clear, actionable insights. With a background in Civil Engineering since 2012, I successfully transitioned to the data field in 2017 and now have 8 years of experience in data analytics and business intelligence.

My diverse background allows me to bring a unique perspective to data challenges, combining technical expertise with domain knowledge and business acumen to help stakeholders make informed decisions.

With a strong foundation in Python, SQL, cloud technologies, and Power BI, I specialize in data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and building scalable data pipelines, as well as developing predictive models using machine learning techniques.

My goal is to help organizations leverage their data to gain competitive advantages, optimize operations, and drive strategic growth through innovative data solutions.
